    • ABOUT US

    Lensa is an event organized by MC111 part 4 students, UiTM Alor Gajah. Lensa is an event that promotes online gaming tournaments for players throughout Malaysia to participate. Lensa event is held annually. So what are you waiting for? Come and join Lensa's tournament for an epic pursuit of the grand champion Lensa tournament title! Who knows? It might be you.

    OBJECTIVES

    Our mission is to encourage a healthy relationship between the university and the community by providing MC111 students with hands-on event management experience. Our event offers the public an amateur e-sports tournament for those who want to play professionally while also promoting awareness of the role of new media in the university's modernization stream.
